About The Podcast

The DPS Podcast is produced by DPS Games. At DPS Games, we want to know what truly makes a game fun. This podcast serves as a way for us to discuss this topic to not only help ourselves, but help other potential game developers as they prepare their own games. We also hope to one day finally determine what DPS truly stands for.

About The Hosts

We are life-long gamers. From the time we started playing games like Dragon Ball Z Budokai, Kingdom Hearts, and Civilization III back in high school, we have always had a dream to make our own game. With our combined skills with programming, music, art, and a little bit of luck, we hope to create games that will help bring joy into the lives of all those who play them.
